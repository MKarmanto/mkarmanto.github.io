Guild info

Fear is a a long lasting WoW-Classic guild on a small server of Flamelash-EU.

We started our journey at the classic launch, build some momentum through phase 1 and have been going strong ever since. We have managed to clear all the content at their relevant tier: MC, BWL, AQ40 and finally Naxxramas

We are a coordinated raiding guild, with a great community and plenty of friendly players to do content with. We pride ourselves in having an atmosphere full of excellent and knowledgeable players, who also enjoy memes, shit and giggles.

If you're looking for more info on the guild, you can pop into our discord or check the webpage for some more information on our kills and on our recruitment needs!

See you in game!
