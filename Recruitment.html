Recruitment

We are looking for friendly and dedicated individuals, who will show up prepared and strive to improve individually while prioritizing the guild's progress before their own. Stay humble, but enjoy min-max and theorycrafting. You are positive and can help maintain a non-toxic and wholesome guild environment. You are expected to behave and treat others like you would want to be treated.
